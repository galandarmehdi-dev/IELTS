How Listening practice works

Audio

Audio starts in test flow

Audio playback starts only after you open a listening test in the app. This page does not autoplay audio.

Environment

Use headphones and a quiet place

For realistic IELTS practice, use headphones, stable internet, and avoid interruptions during listening sections.

Submission

Answers are handled in-platform

Your answers are submitted and reviewed through the IELTS Mock platform, including your normal result/history flow.
