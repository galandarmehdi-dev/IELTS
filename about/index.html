Independent practice platform

IELTS Mock helps students practise under realistic exam conditions.

The platform combines full mock tests, skill-specific practice, saved results, writing review, vocabulary support, and classroom progress tools for IELTS preparation.

What the platform offers

Students can practise Listening, Reading, and Writing through timed exam-style workflows. Teachers and administrators can review results, assign tasks, and follow classroom progress. Public guide pages provide study advice for students who want to prepare more deliberately before entering a timed mock.

IELTS Mock is independent and is not affiliated with IELTS, British Council, IDP Education, or Cambridge Assessment English.

Contact and support

For platform support, account help, or general questions, use the contact page or write to info@ieltsmock.org.
